Data Items to be collected by the mental health professional at each session, on an ongoing management form - Treatment characteristics

    Data items to be collected for all programs

    Item Options Type Notes
    Patient Key   FK - Patient Key

    Each patient should be allocated a 'patient key' which is a unique code to de-identify a patient, allowing him/her to be followed across his/her sessions with the mental health professional, without the evaluators ever needing to know his/her name. Ideally, these codes should be generated and inserted on the form by the Medicare Local, and not by the GP or referrer.

    Required.
    Session date   Past Date

    The date on which the session occurred, must be in the past.

    Required. The column name for this field was previously erroneously listed as session_date..

    The column name for this field was previously erroneously listed as ses_date..
    MHPro name   FK - Referrer or MHPro code

    This is a unique code that is assigned to the mental health professional delivering the session. A coding system for all mental health professionals should be managed by the Medicare Local in order to maintain uniformity.

    Required.
    Statistical Linkages Key   SLK

    As documented at the Australian Government Metadata Online Registry (METeOR), the purpose of the SLK is to maintain greater accuracy of patient data, within and across Medicare Locals. The SLK provides the capacity to identify individual clients across time in order to support more accurate reporting of repeat vs new clients being serviced. Ideally, the SLK should be generated and inserted on the form by the Medicare Local, and not by the referrer.

    Patient data can be entered without an SLK. However, you can not add a referral dated on or after 1st of June, 2013 unless the patient does have an SLK..
    Was a co-payment collected? If so, what was the amount?   Number

    The co-payment is the amount paid by the patient per session, not the fee paid by the project to the provider or the fee paid by the project to the provider plus the patient contribution. In many cases, there will not be a co-payment charged and therefore zero should be entered. Where a co-payment is charged it should be minimal, must be less than $30 and based on an individual's capacity to pay.

    Required, if the session date is on or after the 1st of June, 2014.
    Type of session   FK - Session Type

    The options for type of session available vary depending on which ATAPS program is being delivered. These will appear in a drop down menu specific to each program.

    Required unless the patient did not attend (No show).
    Session duration   FK - Duration

    Indicate the duration of the session from a drop down menu.

    Required unless the patient did not attend (No show).
    Patient did not attend   Boolean

    Tick the 'No show' button if the patient did not attend the session.

    Required, if the session date is on or after the 1st of June, 2013.
    Session Modality   FK - Session Modality

    Session Modality refers to how the session was delivered. Select the modality from the drop down menu.

    Required unless the patient did not attend (No show).
