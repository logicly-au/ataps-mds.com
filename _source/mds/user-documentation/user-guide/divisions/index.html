ATAPS Help - Divisions

Manage PHNs by clicking on the settings icon Â in the top left on any page.

Once on the admin page, click "Divisions".

The next view is of a list of all organisations. Click on the PHNs name to see further details for the PHN. (Clicking on the name of the contact user will take you to the "Manage user" page for that user.)

The division page shows you further details of the PHN, the users associated with it, and the referral types it uses. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the details, or "Delete" to remove the PHN entirely.
