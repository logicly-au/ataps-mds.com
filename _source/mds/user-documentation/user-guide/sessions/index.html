On the referral page, the associated sessions are listed in the "Sessions" box on the right side. To view the details of each individual session, click on the session date to view, or the "edit" link next to it to go directly to the editing view.

Once on the session page, the patient summary box will continue to show on the right, as will the sessions box, indicating which of the sessions you are viewing, and which referral they belong to. The page heading will also give you context as to which session you are editing.

To create a new session, click the "New" link in the top right corner of the sessions box on the referral page. On the subsequent page, complete the form and save.
