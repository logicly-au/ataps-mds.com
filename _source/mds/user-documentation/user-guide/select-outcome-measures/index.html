Selecting Outcome Measures

The 'Select Outcome Measures' page allows organisations to select which Outcome Measures they collect.

Only users with 'Division' level user accounts can access the 'Select Outcome Measures' page.

To navigate to the 'Select Outcome Measures' page:

  • Return to the home page by clicking on the arrow button in the top left corner.
  • In the right hand box, click 'Select Outcome Measures'.

Historically there were many measures available for organisations to collect, however some of these measures were either inappropriate or not commonly used. Pre and post treatment scores for a given consumer on less frequently used outcome measures are not included in statistical analyses for the national evaluation. For these reasons the outcome measures are being standardised and inappropriate or not commonly used ones are being phased out.

Standard Measures

The standard measures are shown at the top of the page. Organisations can turn these measures on or off as appropriate to the organisation.

Non-completion measure

Underneath the standard measures is the Non-Completion measure. There are good reasons why a measure may not be completed i.e. patient refusal. This measure allows for that information to be recorded.Â
