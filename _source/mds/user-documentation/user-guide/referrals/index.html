When viewing a patient record, the patient's referrals will be displayed in the box on the right. Click on the date of the referral to view it, or on the "edit" link beside it to go directly to editing it. You can create a new referral for the patient by clicking the "New" in the top right corner of the referral box.

When you're viewing the referral, on the right side will be a box containing the patient's information for quick reference, and below it, a Sessions box and Measures box containing links to the sessions and measures associated with the referral you're viewing. To edit the referral you're viewing, click the "edit" link next to the referral date in the heading.

To create a new referral, go to the patient record and click the "New" link in the top right corner of the Referrals box on the right. On the subsequent page, select a referral type from the drop-down menu. Once you've selected, a referral form will be displayed for the type you've chosen. Fill out the form and click "Save".

NOTE: AHPro and Referrer codes must be unique. You cannot assign the same code for an AHPro and a Referrer.
