Who to contact

A dedicated Help Desk is available to support Primary Health Networks implementing ATAPS in relation to the minimum dataset system. All enquiries should be directed to: support@ataps-mds.com

If you are involved in the implementation or evaluation of ATAPS, and are seeking evaluation support or advice, feel free to contact the research fellows at the Centre for Health Policy. They have expertise in evaluation and also in working with Primary Health Networks. Please send all queries to the following email address: support@ataps-mds.com
